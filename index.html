Natural Disasters Visualisation

Total number of disasters from 1900 to 2022 based on country

The map shows the number of natural disasters that have occured from 1900 to 2022 per each country. The color of the map is a diverging one where as the color becomes darker the number of disasters are higher as observed in the legend.

Total number of disasters from 1900 to 2022

The graph shows the number of natural disasters that have occured from 1900 to 2022. The trend that can be observed is that until 2002 the yearly number of disasters have increased and then a decrease is observed from there onwards.

Total number of disasters from 1970 to 2021

The bars show the number of natural disasters that have occured from 1970 to 2022. Filtering can be performed by using the drop down to get the statistics based on the continents. The tooltips show the early statistics for each country in the year.
