Overview of Natural Disasters (as of 2021)
Natural Disasters occurred from the year 1900 to 2021

From 1900 to 2021, 8,323,682,171 people were affected by natural disasters throughout world. A total of 128 countries out of 150 experienced some kind of natural disaster during the period. World is prone to earthquakes, flooding, drought, landslides, and avalanches and many more types of disasters. Over hundred years of conflict, coupled with environmental degradation, and insufficient investment in disaster risk reduction strategies, have contributed to increasing vulnerability of the people of the world to cope with the sudden effect of natural disasters.

Number of Natural Disasters per year in each country

The map on the right shows the annual number of natural disasters that have taken place in the year which is represented by the slider. The slider can be adjusted to view data in different years from 1900 to 2021. No natural disasters are represented by light blue.

Number of Natural disasters in each country from 1900 to 2021

The map on the left shows the total number of disasters in each country from 1900 to 2021. The map contains the totals calculated from each country within the period and includes all types of natural disasters that have occurred within the period. All countries with no disasters recorded are observed in light blue.

The most number of natural disasters in this period have occurred in United States of America where a total of 1087 natural disasters have occurred. China is the second highest number of natural disasters with a total of 980 has been observed with in this period. Next in line are India, Philippines and Indonesia respectively, and these are the only other countries with more than 500 natural disasters within this period, based on the data used.

Total Number of Natural disasters from 1900 to 2021

The line chart below represents the total number of disasters occurred in each year from 1900 to 2021. As observed, based on the data in the beginning of the 1900s, the number of natural disasters that have occurred per year is minimal and then gradually increases and eventually peeks in 2002 with 532 natural disasters occurring in the year. Since 2002, a gradual decrease is observed until 2021.

Frequency of the types of Natural Disasters occurred from 1900 to 2021

The radial chart below represents the different types of natural disasters and the frequency in which they have occurred, between the years 1900 and 2021. Based on the data analyzed to create the charts, the most common type of natural disaster is floods which have occurred 5551 time within the period, next in line are storms which have taken place 4496 times in between the years 1900 and 2021.

Number of Natural disasters in each continent from 1900 to 2021

The bar chart on the right represents the total number of natural disasters that have taken place in each continent in the years 1900 to 2021. Each color of each bar represents a specific continent as observed in the color legend. The dropdown selection can be used to filter out separate continents based on the data.

Note: The y-axis changes the scale when filtered to allow better visualization fo the data. When filtered it can be observed that the most number of natural disaster can be observed in Asia followed by America based on the trends observed.
